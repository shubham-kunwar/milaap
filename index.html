Help Tejaswi get a second life

Shares
Share on Whatsapp

Tejaswi, a 20-year girl, pursuing her aeronautical engineering, has met with an accident on 09-02-2021 at Kompally, Hyderabad. Her scooty was hit by an RTC bus from behind. She suffered multiple skull fractures and hemorrhages in the brain.

She is from a poor family, her father is a mason. She was admitted to Yashoda Hospital, Secunderabad. But to our surprise, ArogyaSri (Telangana government free medical card for BPL families) is not accepted here.

We paid 1.5Lakhs and her surgery is underway now. They need immediate financial assistance to save her life.
